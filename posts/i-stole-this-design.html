I Stole this Design

This site was enthusiastically stolen and slightly restyled to meet my needs. The goal is to use it as a playground for writing down ideas and creating prototypes. As I explore the digital medium, I want to display and keep track of my discoveries here.

Wourter de Bres was kind enough to realize that “n00bs” need to steal first, detach, then master. Please visit his original site and read his post about ShuHaRi,the japanese martial arts concept that describes the stages of learning to mastery.

Thank you for reading! If you want to read more, follow me on twitter or read my next post Architecture to UX.
